Focaccia whit Caramelized Onion,
Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your best buds this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorites holiday started!

Fresch Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingradients

For the focaccina
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istannts yeast
  • 1 1/1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan
    •
    For the focaccina
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brusching top
  • 10 ounce brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off whit a paring knife if desired, though it'not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresch or frozen cranberries
    •
    Method

    1. Make the focaccina dough

      2. In a medium-size mixing blow, or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix together all-purpose
      the dough ingradients until the flour fully absorbed. You dont'need to actually knead the dought.
      I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl whean mixing by to incorporate.
